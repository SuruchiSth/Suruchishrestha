Project Plan – 100SMILES Dental Care

Prepared by: Suruchi Shrestha  |  Date: 20 October 2025

Goal

Build a small, clear website for 100SMILES Dental Care that helps new patients find the clinic quickly and book an appointment. My measurable goal is to increase online enquiries/bookings by at least 20% in the first month after launch (measured by email and phone enquiries from the site), and reduce “where are you located?” calls by at least 30% by making address, hours and contact details obvious on every page.

Success Evaluation

I will measure success using simple, practical checks that match the goal:

  • Enquiries count: Track weekly email enquiries sent via the site (contact link) and phone calls from the number on the site (ask reception to tally).
  • Bookings from email promo: Count how many people use the “20% off first visit by email booking” offer in the first month.
  • Findability: Ask two classmates to find “address” and “trading hours” from the home page in under 10 seconds (quick hallway test).

Target Audience

Primary audience: Local families in and around Razole looking for a friendly dental clinic for check-ups, cleaning, emergency care and ongoing preventative care. This includes parents booking for children, young adults, and retirees.

Design choices for this audience:

  • Clear contact & hours on every page: phone number, address, and “Book by email” note in header/footer.
  • Plain language: turn the client’s content into short, scannable sections with headings and bullets.
  • Quick services overview: highlight check-ups, cleanings, implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry and emergency care, with a short “What to expect”.
  • Trust signals: “50+ years’ experience”, long-standing local clinic, and friendly staff introductions with photos.
  • Mobile-friendly: simple layout, readable text, and images compressed for faster loading.

Site Flowchart

My site map shows a simple structure with one Home page linking to four main sections. The link order here is the same order I will use in the site navigation.

Pages

  • Home (index.html): short welcome, key services, offers, address, hours, and strong “Book by email” call-to-action.
  • About Us (about.html): check-ups & cleanings, implants, orthodontics, cosmetic, emergency; brief descriptions and what to expect.
  • Services (service.html): short bios and friendly photos of the dentists/hygienist/receptionist.
  • Articles (articles.html): four articles the client listed; brief summaries with links to external sources if needed.
  • Contact (contact.html): address, map link, phone, email, trading hours, notes about the discounts.

Design & Build Notes

  • Pages & Navigation: Minimum 5 pages, with a consistent header/footer and internal links; external links for the articles as needed.
  • Images: Use only images provided; resize and compress; add meaningful alt text.
  • CSS: Use one external stylesheet; keep colours readable; consistent spacing and headings.
  • Validation: Keep HTML/CSS valid and simple (no frameworks/templates).
  • Submission: Zip site with all files including plan.html and images.
