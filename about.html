Meet Our Dedicated Dental Team

At 100SMILES Dental Care, our experienced and compassionate team is committed to providing personalised, high-quality dental care for patients of all ages. With decades of combined experience and a strong focus on patient comfort, we aim to create healthier smiles for our community.

Our Story

For over three decades, 100SMILES Dental Care has been serving the Razole community with exceptional dental care. Our clinic was built on the vision of delivering gentle, modern, and accessible dentistry for everyone—from young children to seniors.

With more than 50 years of collective experience across our team, we continue to grow and adapt by using advanced dental technologies, ensuring safe, efficient, and comfortable treatments.

About 100SMILES Dental Care

A modern dental care facility located at 33 Fantail Street in Razole for over three decades, our roots in the community run deep. With over 50 years of dental experience, we take pride in providing friendly, reliable, and compassionate care for all patients.

We offer a full spectrum of preventive and restorative dental services including routine wellness check-ups, dental implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, diagnostics, treatments, surgeries, and emergency care. We also prioritise patient education—offering nutritional guidance, oral hygiene advice, and resources to help you understand your dental health.

100SMILES Dental Care is also proud to introduce our new online booking system, designed to make appointment scheduling more convenient. Additionally, we now offer a free callout trial for Dental Check-Up to enhance your experience.

Our promise is to deliver the highest standard of care to patients of all ages, supported by our experienced team of dedicated dentists and friendly staff.
